WELCOME TO BWERANYANGI GIRLS SENIOR SECONDARY SCHOOL

Our Mission

We strive to provide a holistic and inclusive education that fosters critical thinking, creativity, and character development, empowering students to reach their full potential in a dynamic world.

Our Vision

To inspire and nurture young minds to become lifelong learners, ethical leaders, and global citizens who contribute positively to society.

Our Shool Activities

Admission of New Students

Admissions are open for new students. Application forms can be obtained from the school office or downloaded from our website. Entrance examinations and interviews are scheduled as per the admission calendar'

Student Results Picking

Students and parents can collect academic results from the administration office during official working hours. Online result checking is also available through our portal.

Sports

Our school promotes a strong sports culture, offering various activities such as football, basketball, athletics, and more. Students are encouraged to participate in sports to foster teamwork and discipline.

Orientation for New Students

All new students go through an orientation program to familiarize themselves with the school environment, rules, and academic expectations. The program includes guided tours and interactive sessions with teachers and senior students.

Holiday Teaching

We offer holiday teaching programs to help students enhance their knowledge and prepare for upcoming academic challenges. Registration for holiday classes opens at the beginning of each term break.

Student Reporting to School

All students are required to report back to school on the scheduled date after holidays. Late reporting must be communicated in advance to the administration.
